About Us

Crafting Precision Since Day One

Al Haramine Printing & Packaging is a leading press based in Mohammadpur, Rajshahi, specializing in premium labels, hangtags, cartons and agro-product packaging. Built on accurate colour, sharp die-cutting and on-time delivery, our press serves agro-chemical, FMCG and consumer brands across Bangladesh.

From colour separation to die-cutting, every job is proofed in-house before the full run — so what you approve is exactly what ships.
