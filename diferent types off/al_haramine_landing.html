About Us

Where Ink Meets
Precision

Al Haramine Printing & Packaging is a full-service commercial print house equipped with world-class machinery from Germany and Japan. We deliver high-volume offset printing, bespoke packaging design, die cutting, lamination, and label printing — all under one roof.

From mosquito coil boxes to luxury garment packaging, pharmaceutical labels to branded paper bags — our presses run with German precision and Japanese consistency.

What We Do

Full-Spectrum
Print Services

🖨️
Offset Printing

High-volume CMYK offset printing with Heidelberg and Hashimoto presses. Up to 12,000 sheets per hour with consistent color accuracy.

✂️
Die Cutting

Precision die cutting for custom box shapes, blister packs, and intricate packaging forms. Three dedicated die cutting machines for fast turnaround.

✨
Lamination

Gloss, matte, and soft-touch lamination to elevate your packaging's finish and durability. Protects print and adds premium tactile quality.

🏷️
Label Printing

Dedicated label printing for pharmaceutical, food, and consumer products. Precise registration and vibrant color reproduction at scale.

📦
Box Packaging

Complete packaging solutions from structural design to finished folding cartons. Medicines, garments, cosmetics, and food packaging.

🛍️
Paper Bags

Branded paper bags with full-bleed printing for retail, restaurants, and events. Durable construction with premium print quality.
