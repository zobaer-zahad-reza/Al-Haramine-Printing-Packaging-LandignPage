What We Do

End-to-End Printing
& Packaging Solutions

From first print to finished product — our facility handles every stage of production under one roof.

🖨️
Offset Printing

Multi-color, high-fidelity printing using Heidelberg and Hashimoto presses. Sharp registration, vibrant inks, and consistent quality across large runs.

📦
Packaging Boxes

Custom-designed folding cartons and rigid boxes for panjabis, creams, coils, electronics, and consumer goods — with precise die-cutting and scoring.

🏷️
Product Labels

Dedicated label printing for agricultural chemicals, cosmetics, food products, and pharmaceuticals — including Bengali and bilingual artwork.

✂️
Die Cutting

Three dedicated die-cutting machines for precise shape cutting, creasing, and perforation — turning printed sheets into production-ready packaging blanks.

✨
Lamination

Gloss, matte, and soft-touch lamination finishes that protect print surfaces and elevate the perceived quality of your packaging.

🛍️
Carry Bags & Branding

Printed carry bags, retail bags, and branded packaging for restaurants, fashion outlets, and FMCG brands — ready for delivery or export.
